        % \begin{solutionordottedlines}[\stretch{3}]

        % \end{solutionordottedlines}

\newprob{1715831262}
{
    一個熱容量為 \hc{660} 的電水壼盛載着\oc{15}，2.7 kg的水。電水壼提供的功率是2500 W，假設沒有能量損失於周圍環境，計算出30分鐘之後壼內水的質量。\zzh{2}
    % \par A kettle of heat capacity \hc{660}contains 2.7kg of water at \oc{15}. If the power supplied to the kettle is 2500W, and assume no heat loss to surroundings, calculate the mass of water left in the kettle after 30 minutes.
}{
    \sol
    水壼吸收的能量Energy absorbed by the kettle = $C\Delta T=660\times (100-15)=56100\unit{J}$.\par
    水壼提供的能量Total energy supplied = $2500\times 30\times 60=4500000$ J\par
    設水壼中剩下的水的質量為$m_w$。Let $m_w$ be mass of water left in the kettle.
    \begin{align*}
        4500000 & =(2.7-m)\times \num{2.26e6}+2.7\times 4200\times (100-15)+56100 \\
        m       & =1.16\unit{kg}
    \end{align*}
    水壼中剩下1.16 kg的水。\par
    1.16 kg of water left in the kettle。
}

\newprob{1715831461}
{
    湯姆將0.35 kg，\oc{25}的橙汁倒入杯子中。他準備了一些相同的冰塊，每個冰塊質量為15 g。他最少要加多少冰塊才能製作出溫度不超過 \oc{5}的冷飲？忽略杯子的熱容量和杯子和周圍環境之間的熱交換。(橙汁的比熱容量= \shc{5000}；水的比熱容量= \shc{4200}；冰的比熱容量= \shc{2260}；冰的融解比潛熱= \slh{3.34e5})
    % \par Tommy pours 0.35kg of orange juice at room temperature of \oc{25}into a glass. Some identical ice cubes each of mass 15g are availabe. At least how many ice cubes should he add to the juice in order to produce a cold drink with temperature not higher than \oc{5}? Neglect the heat capacity of the glass and heat exchange between the glass and surroundings. (specific heat capacity of orange = \shc{5000}; specific heat capacity of water = \shc{4200}; specific latent heat of fusion of ice = \slh{3.34e5})
}{
    \sol
    設n為冰塊數目。 Let n be the number of ice cubes used.
    \par 橙汁提供的熱＝冰熔化時吸收的熱＋熔解後水吸收的熱
    \par Heat given out by juice = heat absorbed by ice in melting + heat absorbed by water from melted ice.

    \[mc\Delta T=m_o\ell_f+m_oc_w\Delta T\]
    \[0.35\times 5000\times (25-5)=(n\times 0.015)\times \num{3.34e5}+(n\times 0.014)\times 4200 \times (5-0)\]
    \[\Rightarrow 35000=5325n\Rightarrow n=6.6\]
    所以他需要加7塊冰。 He should add 7 ice cubes.
}
